Different Types of Lions

Asiatic Lion

The Asiatic lion, also known as the Indian lion and the Persian lion, is a population of Panthera leo leo that today survives in the wild only in India.

Barbory Lion

The Barbary lion, also called the North African lion, Berber lion, Atlas lion, and Egyptian lion, is an extinct population of the lion subspecies Panthera leo leo.
